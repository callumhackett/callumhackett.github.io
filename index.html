Music in Pieces

Music in Pieces is a limited series podcast about the je ne sais quoi of classical music. Currently in production, each episode will focus on a different aspect of musical structure, with plenty of examples and interviews with experts. Newcomers will find out what the genre does uniquely well, and aficionados will hear the music anew.
