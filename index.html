Callum Hackett

I’m a researcher in human and artificial intelligence interested in the relationship between language and knowledge. Drawing on cognitive linguistics, computer science and philosophy, I support the view that intelligence is not reducible to data processing and that AI is a cultural technology that does not think or feel.

Writing

I’m currently drafting a book on pseudoscience in the AI industry and I write Culture & Anarchy, a newsletter about modern humanism viewed through the lenses of naturalism, pragmatism and Daoism. I’ve also published academic work on the biological origins of language.

Consulting
