NATURAL

I used to be a researcher and lecturer in linguistics, specializing in formal syntax and the evolution of linguistic ability in our species. Today I still write on the science of natural language, with a focus on explaining how the field has curiously managed to make great progress while also having very shaky philosophical foundations. My lodestars are Chomsky and Wittgenstein and my hope is to reconcile what seems irreconcilable about them.
